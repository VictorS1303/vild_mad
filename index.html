Foraging is like a treasure hunt in nature! It's finding yummy wild treats like berries and elderflower. We learn about different flavors and how to be friends with the earth. Foraging is like nature's special grocery store, where we take only what we need and say thank you to the plants. It's a fun way to explore and make sure nature stays happy and healthy!
